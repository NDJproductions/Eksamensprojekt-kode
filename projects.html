What Have I Worked On?

One of the things I have worked on during my time as a student of multimediadesign is product placement and foto editing.

The editing is done mostly via Adobe photoshop, which I have become very familiar with during this time. And it is something I can offer to businesses as well.

What I highlighted in the previous project has come more to fruition thanks to learning about general photography.

I`ve come to understand more about cameras, their settings, lighting and the setup needed to take great pictures. It is a skillset I am happy to have learned and look forward to evolve.

Throughout the 1st semester, a great deal of our projects have been designed and developed via Figma.

This has given me a lot of experience and knowhow, not only in prototyping, but also in how these programs work, and what to do to make the most of them. Figma especially has become a stable in my workflow.

The Fabrication Laboratory at UCL has opened my eyes and given me access to new and interesting means of creating physical products.

By using programs such as Adobe Illustrator, I've created and participated in making several physical prototypes. Some for my own personal use and others for school projects. This means that I have a license to be in and operate in Fab Lab's all over the world.

Aside from Photoshop, Illustrator and Figma, I have experience in programs such as Adobe AfterEffects as well. Creating and editing are a part of my skills in the world of media.

I have a good understanding of storyboarding and the principles behind animation and I always try to bring in a personal touch to my work.
